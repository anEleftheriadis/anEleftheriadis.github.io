Water Coaster

Stay Hydrated with a Splash of Technology

A smart coaster designed to help users stay hydrated by measuring the
amount of water in their cup or glass. The coaster sends real-time data to an accompanying app, allowing users to track their water intake throughout the day.

How it Works

Connect the App and the Water Coaster via Bluetooth.

Follow the instructions of the App & calibrate your first cup/glass.

Features

Innovative

Its smart features revolutionize how you stay hydrated, making it easy and enjoyable to prioritize your health.

Easy Setup

Simply connect the smart coaster to the companion app, calibrate the first cup/glass and you’ll be ready to start tracking your hydration.

Hydration Reminders

Customized reminders from the app, ensure you never miss a chance to take a sip and maintain optimal hydration levels.

Cup Compatibility

The smart coaster supports various cup sizes, glasses, and water bottles, offering versatility to fit your preferred hydration containers.

Health Insights

Gain valuable insights into your hydration patterns and their impact on your overall well-being.

Ideal Gift Option

Water Coaster makes for a thoughtful gift for loved ones, colleagues, or employees, promoting health-consciousness in their lives.

Hydration Statistics

The app stores and presents your historical water intake data, enabling you to analyze trends and identify opportunities for improvement in your hydration habits.

Waterproof

Water Coaster’s waterproof feature provides peace of mind against spills and ensures long-lasting durability.

Our Questionnaire

In the questionnaire conducted a few months back, we reached out to our community to gather firsthand perspectives on hydration, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

Read more

The questionnaire delved into critical aspects of someone’s daily water consumption, shedding light on their hydration habits and challenges. We sought these valuable insights on the difficulty of remembering to drink water throughout the day and the significance of a smart product that acts as a supportive reminder. By exploring these key elements, we gained a profound understanding of your hydration needs.
